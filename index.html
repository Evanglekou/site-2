Queens Beauty App

To revolutionize how our clients interact with cosmetics, we have launched a proprietary mobile application on the Google Play Store.

The app bridges e-commerce with augmented reality, allowing users to digitally try on makeup, collect studio loyalty points, and purchase perfumes directly from their smartphones.

  • AR Virtual Try-On: Test lipsticks and eyeshadows using your device's camera.
  • Digital Queens Club: Scan your mobile card in-studio to earn beauty rewards.
  • E-Commerce: Order premium toiletries and perfumes for home delivery.
  • Studio Bookings: Reserve personal care consultations instantly.
