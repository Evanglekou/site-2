Psarianos Fabric Connect

To modernize the wholesale supply chain, STELIOS CHR. PSARIANOS & GKITSA IFIGENEIA P.C. has launched a proprietary B2B mobile application.

The Fabric Connect app is designed for tailors, fashion designers, and retail buyers. It digitizes our entire warehouse catalog, allowing clients to scan physical fabric swatches and instantly order bulk rolls via their Android devices.

  • Swatch Scanner: Scan QR codes on physical fabric samples to view specs.
  • Live Inventory: Check warehouse stock levels for piece goods in real-time.
  • B2B Ordering: Place secure wholesale orders for apparel and notions.
  • Logistics Tracking: Track the delivery of your fabric rolls directly to your atelier.
